## Description

Medical bills are confusing by design. Claro Lens gives you the same tools professional billing advocates use — powered by AI — to understand, verify, and dispute every charge.

**SCAN ANY HEALTH DOCUMENT**
Point your camera at a medical bill, Explanation of Benefits (EOB), lab result, insurance card, or discharge summary. Claro reads it and tells you exactly what it means in plain English.

**INSTANT AI ANALYSIS**
Every document is analyzed by Claude AI, trained as a medical billing expert. You get:
• A plain-English summary of what the document says
• Every line item decoded from medical jargon
• 🟢 What looks correct and favorable
• 🟡 Items worth a closer look
• 🔴 Billing errors that need immediate action

**CATCH COSTLY BILLING ERRORS**
Claro actively looks for the most common mistakes:
• Duplicate charges
• Unbundling and upcoding
• Balance billing violations
• Incorrect deductible or copay application
• Charges for services not rendered
• Rates exceeding usual and customary amounts

**ONE-TAP DISPUTE LETTERS**
When errors are found, generate a professional ready-to-mail dispute letter in seconds. Claro cites the No Surprises Act, state balance billing protections, and applicable patient rights — the same arguments a billing attorney would make.

**TRACK YOUR COSTS**
• Monitor your deductible and out-of-pocket max progress in real time
• See your annual spending broken down by document type
• Separate profiles for your whole family

**PRIVACY FIRST**
Your medical records never leave your device. Document images are sent only to a secure AI proxy for analysis and are never stored on any server.
